About

The picturesque state of Himachal Pradesh is nestled between the snow topped Himalayan ranges in North India. Known for its spectacular and panoramic landscapes, this traveler's paradise is blessed with lofty snow peaks, verdant valleys, thundering rivers, enchanting mountain lakes, flower carpeted meadows, ancient temples, and magnificent monasteries steeped in time. Himachal Pradesh proves to be a wonderful location for the tourists who want to relax. The state also offers amazing opportunities for trekking, mountaineering, para gliding, skiing, river rafting and ice skating
